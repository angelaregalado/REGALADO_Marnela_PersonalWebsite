A Bit About Me

I am an aspiring digital artist still struggling with art, but I give my all for every piece I create. In fact, I have been improving quite a lot these past few days, if I say so myself. I also write some stories, which usually accompany the pieces I had drawn. But I love writing poems much more than I love writing stories. There’s something about them that I couldn’t quite put my finger on. I don’t consider myself a romantic, though.

In order to be able to provide ideas I can use for writing and drawing, I read a lot of books, novels especially. I mostly read about post-apocalyptic, dystopian fantasies, stories revolving around mythologies, and conspiracy novels.

I also love to learn, although I admit I am not as fast as others. But I do try my best and work hard.
